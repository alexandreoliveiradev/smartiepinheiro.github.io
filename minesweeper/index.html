Minesweeper
Pins left: 20
The objective of the game is to locate all of the 20 hidden mines. The numbers on the cells tells you how many mines that cell is touching.
Left click on a cell if you believe it doesn't hold a mine. Right click on a cell if you believe it does hold a mine to pin it. If you believe you wrongly pinned it just right click again to unpin it.
You lose when a cell that you left click open contains a mine. You win if you either pin all the mines or right click on all the non-mine cells.
