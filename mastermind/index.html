Mastermind
The goal of Mastermind is to discover a combination of 4 colours selected by the computer at random.
At each turn, you make your own 4 color combination guess (colors can be repeated).
The right column gives you how many colors are part of the solution and on the right position.
The wrong column gives you how many are part of the solution but on the wrong position.
